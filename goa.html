The 5-Day Roadmap: Goa

A perfectly balanced journey through the tropical heart of India. Click on the locations below to find them on Google Maps.

Day 01

Coastal Arrival & North Goa Pulse

Arrive in the vibrant North. Settle into your boutique stay before catching a legendary sunset and exploring the high-energy beach shacks.

Baga Beach Calangute Tito's Lane
Day 02

Latin Quarters & Old Goa Heritage

Step into the Portuguese era. Wander the colorful streets of Fontainhas followed by the spiritual silence of Old Goa's ancient cathedrals.

Fontainhas Basilica of Bom Jesus Panjim
Day 03

Nature’s Majesty: Dudhsagar Falls

A day for the wild. Journey through the Bhagwan Mahavir Sanctuary to witness the spectacular "Sea of Milk" cascading down the Western Ghats.

Dudhsagar Falls Mollem National Park
Day 04

South Goa Serenity & White Sands

Escape to the quiet side. Relax on pristine crescent-shaped beaches, enjoy quiet backwater kayaking, and dine under the starlit sky.

Palolem Beach Agonda Cabo de Rama
Day 05

Artisanal Markets & Cliffside Farewells

Final day for souvenir shopping and bohemian vibes. End your journey with fine Greek dining on the cliffs overlooking the Arabian Sea.

Anjuna Flea Market Vagator Cliffs Thalassa

Famous Places to Go

A curated selection of Goa's most iconic landmarks, from historic cathedrals to hidden waterfalls.

Heritage

Basilica of Bom Jesus

A UNESCO World Heritage site holding the mortal remains of St. Francis Xavier. A masterpiece of Baroque architecture.

Nature

Dudhsagar Falls

One of India's tallest waterfalls. The "Sea of Milk" cascades through the lush Western Ghats in a breathtaking display.

History

Fort Aguada

A 17th-century Portuguese fort standing guard over the Arabian Sea. Famous for its lighthouse and panoramic sunset views.

Iconic Places to Eat

Taste the legendary flavors of Goa, ranging from sunset Greek taverns to traditional family-run kitchens.

Sunset Dining

Thalassa

The legendary Greek tavern at Vagator. Famous for its spectacular sunsets, fire dances, and vibrant atmosphere.

Authentic

Mum’s Kitchen

A Panjim landmark preserving traditional Goan recipes passed down through generations. True Goan soul food.

Boutique Garden

Gunpowder

Set in a beautiful Assagao bungalow, this spot serves incredible South Indian flavors with a bohemian vibe.

How to Get Around

Navigate the city with ease using these reliable transport options tailored for your comfort.

Private Cabs

The gold standard for urban mobility. Utilize app-based services for pre-fixed fares and air-conditioned comfort, or book a private chauffeur for full-day heritage explorations.

Public Bus

Experience the city like a local. Efficient AC bus networks connect historic districts with modern hubs, offering the most sustainable and budget-friendly way to traverse the city.

Metro & Trains

Beat the peak-hour rush. Modern rapid transit systems provide lightning-fast connectivity across the city, allowing you to move between distant landmarks in record time.
