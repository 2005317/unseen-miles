Destination Deep Dive

Goa.

The tropical soul of India, where heritage meets the horizon.

Famous Places to Go

A curated selection of Goa's most iconic landmarks, from historic cathedrals to hidden waterfalls.

Heritage

Basilica of Bom Jesus

A UNESCO World Heritage site holding the mortal remains of St. Francis Xavier. A masterpiece of Baroque architecture.

Nature

Dudhsagar Falls

One of India's tallest waterfalls. The "Sea of Milk" cascades through the lush Western Ghats in a breathtaking display.

History

Fort Aguada

A 17th-century Portuguese fort standing guard over the Arabian Sea. Famous for its lighthouse and panoramic sunset views.

Culture

Fontainhas

The Latin Quarter of Panjim. Wander through narrow winding streets lined with brightly colored colonial-style houses.

Iconic Places to Eat

Taste the legendary flavors of Goa, ranging from sunset Greek taverns to traditional family-run kitchens.

Sunset Dining

Thalassa

The legendary Greek tavern at Vagator. Famous for its spectacular sunsets, fire dances, and vibrant atmosphere.

Authentic

Mum’s Kitchen

A Panjim landmark preserving traditional Goan recipes passed down through generations. True Goan soul food.

Boutique Garden

Gunpowder

Set in a beautiful Assagao bungalow, this spot serves incredible South Indian flavors with a bohemian vibe.

Seafood

Fisherman’s Wharf

Experience the riverside charm of South Goa with the freshest local catches and a classic coastal ambiance.
