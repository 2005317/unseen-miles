Famous Places to Go

Experience the timeless rituals and ancient architecture that make Kashi the spiritual capital of India.

Ritual & Light

Dashashwamedh Ghat

The main ghat and the site of the legendary evening Ganga Aarti. A spectacular ceremony of fire, bells, and chants that has occurred daily for centuries.

Holy Pilgrimage

Kashi Vishwanath Temple

One of the twelve holiest Jyotirlingas. Dedicated to Lord Shiva, the Golden Temple is the beating heart of the city's spiritual identity.

Life & Eternity

Manikarnika Ghat

The primary cremation ghat of Varanasi. A place where the circle of life and death is witnessed with profound perspective and ancient dignity.

Iconic Places to Eat

Delve into the street food culture of Banaras, famous for its unique chaats, traditional sweets, and legendary lassis.

Global Icon

Blue Lassi Shop

A tiny heritage shop in the lanes near Manikarnika Ghat. Famous for serving over 80 varieties of hand-churned lassi in clay pots (kulhads).

Legendary Chaat

Deena Chat Bhandar

The definitive spot for Tamatar Chaat and Palak Chaat. A culinary ritual for anyone visiting the Luxa Road area.

Traditional Thali

Shree Shivay

Authentic Banarasi vegetarian dining at its finest. Known for its multi-course Thali that represents the sattvic soul of the city.

How to Get Around

Navigate the city with ease using these reliable transport options tailored for your comfort.

Private Cabs

The gold standard for urban mobility. Utilize app-based services for pre-fixed fares and air-conditioned comfort, or book a private chauffeur for full-day heritage explorations.

Public Bus

Experience the city like a local. Efficient AC bus networks connect historic districts with modern hubs, offering the most sustainable and budget-friendly way to traverse the city.

Metro & Trains

Beat the peak-hour rush. Modern rapid transit systems provide lightning-fast connectivity across the city, allowing you to move between distant landmarks in record time.
