Kolkata.

Where colonial history meets artistic soul and timeless hospitality.

The 5-Day Roadmap

A soulful immersion into Kolkata's heritage, culture, and iconic riverside spirits.

Day 01

Colonial Grandeur & Park Street

Experience the majestic white marble of Victoria Memorial and the festive pulse of Central Kolkata.

Victoria Memorial Park Street
Day 02

Riverside Soul & Iconic Bridges

Cross the Howrah Bridge and witness the sunrise flower market before a sunset boat ride at Princep Ghat.

Howrah Bridge Princep Ghat
Day 03

Spirituality & Potter's Hands

Visit Dakshineswar Kali Temple followed by the artisan lanes of Kumartuli to see idol makers at work.

Dakshineswar Kumartuli
Day 04

Intellectual Vibe & Museums

Wander the world's largest second-hand book market at College Street and visit the Indian Museum.

College Street Indian Museum
Day 05

Heritage Markets & Reflection

Conclude with a visit to the Mother House and souvenir hunting in the historic New Market area.

New Market Mother House

Famous Places to Go

Milestones of architecture and engineering that define the Kolkata skyline.

Grandeur

Victoria Memorial

A marble masterpiece built in the early 20th century, now home to a stunning museum.

Engineering

Howrah Bridge

The sixth-longest cantilever bridge in the world and the gateway to the city.

Heritage

Dakshineswar Temple

An ancient riverside temple dedicated to Goddess Kali, rich in spiritual history.

Iconic Places to Eat

Taste the legendary flavors of the city, from Mughlai Biryani to affordable street classics.

Biryani Legend

Arsalan

The gold standard for Kolkata-style Biryani with that iconic melting potato.

Continental Hub

Peter Cat

Home of the legendary Chelo Kebab and a distinct old-world Park Street vibe.

Pocket Friendly

Raju Da (Porota Shop)

Famous "tinte porota" and unlimited sabzi for only 30 rupees. A true local secret.

How to Get Around

Navigate the city with ease using these reliable transport options tailored for your comfort.

Private Cabs

The gold standard for urban mobility. Utilize app-based services for pre-fixed fares and air-conditioned comfort, or book a private chauffeur for full-day heritage explorations.

Public Bus

Experience the city like a local. Efficient AC bus networks connect historic districts with modern hubs, offering the most sustainable and budget-friendly way to traverse the city.

Metro & Trains

Beat the peak-hour rush. Modern rapid transit systems provide lightning-fast connectivity across the city, allowing you to move between distant landmarks in record time.
