The 5-Day Roadmap: Jaipur

Step through time in the capital of Rajasthan—where royal forts meet celestial observatories and the desert soul comes alive. Click the locations to view them on Google Maps.

Day 01

Arrival & The Heart of the Pink City

Settle into a heritage haveli. Spend your first afternoon witnessing the honeycomb facade of the Palace of Breeze and wandering through the chaotic, colorful lanes of the old bazaars.

Hawa Mahal Johari Bazaar
Day 02

The Royal Hillside Trio

Journey up the Aravalli hills. Marvel at the shimmering Sheesh Mahal in Amber Fort, followed by panoramic city views from Nahargarh and the military might of Jaigarh.

Amer Fort Nahargarh Fort Jaigarh Fort
Day 03

Celestial Science & Royal Courtyards

Explore the scientific genius of the 18th century at Jantar Mantar, followed by the opulent City Palace. Conclude at the Albert Hall Museum as it glows under evening lights.

City Palace Jantar Mantar Albert Hall
Day 04

Spiritual Valleys & Artisan Soul

Visit the sacred Monkey Temple at dawn. Spend your afternoon in Sanganer witnessing traditional block printing, and stop by the vibrant Patrika Gate for a photographic feast.

Galta Ji Temple Patrika Gate
Day 05

Heritage Leisure & Royal Farewells

Final day for souvenir hunting in the Bapu Bazaar. Conclude with a visit to the serene Water Palace before your departure from the royal lands.

Bapu Bazaar Jal Mahal

Famous Places to Go

Step through time in the capital of Rajasthan, where every stone tells a story of valor and artistry.

Grandeur

Amer Fort

A magnificent 16th-century fortress overlooking Maota Lake. Famous for its artistic Hindu-style elements and the shimmering Sheesh Mahal.

Iconic Architecture

Hawa Mahal

The 'Palace of Breeze'. Its unique honeycomb facade with 953 small windows was built to allow royal ladies to watch street festivals unseen.

UNESCO Science

Jantar Mantar

The world's largest stone astronomical observatory. A collection of nineteen architectural astronomical instruments built in 1734.

Iconic Places to Eat

From royal thalis to world-class aesthetics, discover the flavors that define Jaipur.

Cultural Immersion

Chokhi Dhani

An ethnic village resort offering a deep dive into Rajasthani life, complete with traditional dance, crafts, and legendary royal thalis.

Heritage Sweet Shop

Laxmi Mishtan Bhandar

Located in the heart of Johari Bazaar. Famous for its Ghevar, Pyaaz Kachori, and the royal Rajasthani Thali experience.

Aesthetic Dining

Bar Palladio

An exquisite blue-themed Italian bar within the Narain Niwas Palace. Where Venetian luxury meets the spirit of the Orient.

How to Get Around

Navigate the city with ease using these reliable transport options tailored for your comfort.

Private Cabs

The gold standard for urban mobility. Utilize app-based services for pre-fixed fares and air-conditioned comfort, or book a private chauffeur for full-day heritage explorations.

Public Bus

Experience the city like a local. Efficient AC bus networks connect historic districts with modern hubs, offering the most sustainable and budget-friendly way to traverse the city.

Metro & Trains

Beat the peak-hour rush. Modern rapid transit systems provide lightning-fast connectivity across the city, allowing you to move between distant landmarks in record time.
