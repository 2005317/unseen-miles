Famous Places to Go

Experience the "Silicon Valley" pulse through its royal palaces, botanical gardens, and vibrant public spaces.

Nature & Heritage

Lalbagh Botanical Garden

Commissioned by Hyder Ali in 1760. Famous for its magnificent glass house modeled after London's Crystal Palace.

Royal Tudor

Bangalore Palace

A breathtaking Tudor-style palace inspired by Windsor Castle. Explore intricate wood carvings and manicured grounds.

Lush Green

Cubbon Park

The lungs of the city. A 300-acre oasis housing the iconic red-brick State Central Library.

Iconic Places to Eat

From legendary 1920s tiffin rooms to the vibrant craft brewery culture.

Legacy Tiffin

MTR (Mavalli Tiffin Rooms)

A heritage culinary landmark since 1924. The birthplace of the Rava Idli and old Bangalore breakfast traditions.

The Best Dosa

Vidyarthi Bhavan

Located in Gandhi Bazaar. Watch waiters carry stacks of crispy Benne Dosa in this legendary eatery.

Craft Brewery

Toit Brewpub

The pulse of Indiranagar. A pioneer in Bangalore's craft beer revolution, known for its incredible atmosphere.

How to Get Around

Navigate the city with ease using these reliable transport options tailored for your comfort.

Private Cabs

The gold standard for urban mobility. Utilize app-based services for pre-fixed fares and air-conditioned comfort, or book a private chauffeur for full-day heritage explorations.

Public Bus

Experience the city like a local. Efficient AC bus networks connect historic districts with modern hubs, offering the most sustainable and budget-friendly way to traverse the city.

Metro & Trains

Beat the peak-hour rush. Modern rapid transit systems provide lightning-fast connectivity across the city, allowing you to move between distant landmarks in record time.
